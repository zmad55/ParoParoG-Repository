The COVAX Facility — co-led by Gavi PERSON , the Coalition for Epidemic Preparedness Innovations ORG (CEPI) and the World Health Organization ORG (WHO), together with UNICEF — aims to provide access to quality-assured COVID-19 vaccines, enabling the protection of frontline health care and social workers, as well as other high-risk and vulnerable groups such as the elderly and those with underlying medical conditions.
