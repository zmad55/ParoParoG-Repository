National and international experts who are part of the WHO ORG and UNDP ORG support to the Philippines GPE call on the national government, local governments, public and private sector partners to promote mental health with the following recommendations: ( 1 CARDINAL ) strengthen mental health leadership and governance; ( 2 CARDINAL ) invest in evidence-based, cost-effective clinical and population-based mental health interventions; ( 3 CARDINAL ) include mental health interventions while extending UHC ORG ; ( 4 CARDINAL ) increase the capacity of the health-care workforce and the health system to provide mental health; and ( 5 CARDINAL ) Invest in mental health research and development.
